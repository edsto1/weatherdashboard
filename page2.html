Latitude VS Temp



In this visualization, we notice that weather becomes increasingly warmer near the equator, as one might expect. It is also notable that cities south of the equator tend to be warmer than those north of the equator.

Latitude VS Humidity



When looking at the humidity of cities around the world and their relation to the equator, cities on or within extreme proximity to it tend to have high humidity rates. Beyond that however, equatorial proximity does not seem to have a definitive effect on a city's humidity levels.

Latitude VS Cloudiness



As one might expect, a city's proximity to the equator does not hold a large bearing on its cloudiness. This graph shows that levels of cloudiness rather even across hemispheres.

Latitude VS Windspeed



The level of wind speed a city experiences does look to have some relation to its proximity to the equator. This plot indicates that cities north of the equator tend to have higher levels of wind speed.
